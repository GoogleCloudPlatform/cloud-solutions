# Award-Winning Video Ad Patterns

## Apple "Shot on iPhone" Pattern
- Act 1: Ordinary moment, extraordinary beauty
- Act 2: Camera reveals scale/detail
- Act 3: Simple text, product, silence
- Key: Let the visually stunning speak — minimal voiceover

## Nike "Just Do It" Pattern
- Act 1: Athlete at rest, tension building
- Act 2: Explosive action, slow-motion impact
- Act 3: Achievement moment, brand tagline
- Key: Emotional build-up, cathartic release

## Porsche "Precision" Pattern
- Act 1: Detail macro — material texture, engineering
- Act 2: Product in motion — speed, grace, environment
- Act 3: Hero shot — product at rest, brand logo, silence
- Key: Let craftsmanship tell the story

## Dyson "Problem-Solution" Pattern
- Act 1: Slow-motion problem visualization
- Act 2: Product tech in action (cutaway, x-ray view)
- Act 3: Clean result, satisfaction, brand
- Key: Science-meets-design aesthetic

## Google "Everyday Magic" Pattern
- Act 1: Real person, relatable situation
- Act 2: Product seamlessly helps
- Act 3: Warm smile, shared moment, brand
- Key: Technology that disappears into daily life

## Music and Sound Design
- Lyria music should BUILD across acts: ambient start → rhythmic middle → emotional peak
- Voiceover volume 100%, music at 15% — music supports, never competes
- Final 2 seconds: music resolves, voiceover lands the tagline
