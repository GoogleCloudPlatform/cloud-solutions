# SecureVision — NestGuard Pro Marketing Guide

## Brand Identity

- **Brand Name:** SecureVision
- **Tagline:** "Always Watching. Always Caring."
- **Brand Voice:** Reassuring, modern, trustworthy.
    Not fear-based — focus on peace of mind.
- **Color Palette:** Clean White (#FFFFFF), Slate Blue
    (#4A6FA5), Soft Gray (#E8E8E8), Accent Green (#34C759)

## Product: NestGuard Pro Smart Home Camera

- **Category:** Smart Home / Security
- **Price:** $149.99 (MSRP $179.99)
- **Key Features:**
    - 4K HDR video with night vision
    - 360-degree pan and tilt
    - AI person/pet/package detection
    - Two-way audio with noise cancellation
    - Local + cloud storage options
    - Works with Google Home, Alexa, Apple HomeKit
- **Differentiator:** Privacy-first design — physical
    lens cover, on-device AI processing, no mandatory
    subscription

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** Homeowners 28-50, tech-savvy, value privacy
- **Secondary:** Parents with young children, pet owners
- **Psychographics:** Values safety without surveillance
    anxiety, prefers brands that respect data privacy

## Visual Direction

- Clean, bright, airy home environments (not dark/scary)
- Show product in real home settings — living room shelf,
    nursery, front door area
- Lifestyle shots with families, pets — warm and welcoming
- Never show surveillance-style footage or fear-based imagery

## Campaign Themes

1.  "Peace of Mind" — Focus on knowing your home
    is safe while you are away
1.  "Smart, Not Creepy" — Privacy-first messaging
    that differentiates from competitors
1.  "Your Home, Your Rules" — No mandatory
    subscriptions, local storage option

## Competitors

- Ring Indoor Cam ($59.99 — cheaper but
    subscription-dependent)
- Arlo Pro 5 ($249.99 — premium tier)
- Google Nest Cam ($179.99 — ecosystem lock-in)
